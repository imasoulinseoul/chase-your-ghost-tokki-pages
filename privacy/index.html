Privacy Policy

Chase your ghost, Tokki · Last updated: 5 July 2026

Overview

Chase your ghost, Tokki (“the App”) is a watchOS fitness application. This policy describes how the App handles information on your Apple Watch.

Information the App uses

Location (GPS)
The App uses your location only while you are actively recording or racing a route. GPS is not used continuously on idle menu screens. Location data is used to draw your route, measure distance and pace, and support ghost races on saved courses.

Health and fitness data
With your permission, the App reads heart rate, cadence, distance, and related workout metrics from Apple Health during runs. With your permission, completed workouts and GPS routes may be written to Apple Health.

On-device storage

Saved routes, run history, and workout details are stored locally on your Apple Watch. The App does not operate a cloud account or sync your data to our servers.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data you choose to save to Apple Health is governed by Apple’s policies and your Health app settings.

Third parties

The App does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs in its current release.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will change when we do.
